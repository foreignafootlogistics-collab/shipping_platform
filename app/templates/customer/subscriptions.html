How Subscription Coverage Works
Packages valued at US$100 or less
  • Freight is covered.
  • Handling is covered.
  • Customs charges are covered.
  • EPC/bad-address and other additional charges still apply.
Packages valued over US$100
  • Freight is covered.
  • Handling is covered.
  • Full customs charges apply.
  • EPC/bad-address and other additional charges still apply.

Subscription usage is based on rounded-up billable weight. A package must remain within the plan’s per-package and remaining total allowance.
